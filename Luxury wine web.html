Our Story

A Legacy of Winemaking Excellence

Founded in 1978 in the heart of Napa Valley, Noir has been dedicated to producing wines of exceptional quality and character. Our philosophy is simple: great wine begins in the vineyard.

Through sustainable practices and minimal intervention, we allow the true expression of each terroir to shine in every bottle. Each vintage tells the story of its origin, captured through our winemaker's artistry.

Antoine Moreau

Master Winemaker
